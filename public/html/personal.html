Rainbow Six Siege


I've been playing siege since 2018, Starting on Xbox with friends, and playing awfully, then making the jump to PC in 2019 where I quickly picked up the mechanics. I own multiple accounts, including a level 240 one, however I switched to playing on a ubisoft copy of the game (rather than steam).

I have achieved top 1% multiple times, although I play much less than I once did, with only a couple games a season at this point. It still remains a big part of my life, with my hours across the 3 accounts coming close to 3.5k. Im a massive fan of competitive tactical shooters, like CSGO, Siege, and Others. Siege especially is build on an instant kill headshot with any weapon, making the time to kill as fast as your reaction times with a well placed shot, allowing for many playstyles to work, from fast paced gameplay designed to throw off your enemies aim, or slower anchor playstyles using crosshair placement and utility to slow your opponent and get easy picks.

Siege is unique in this way, as many shooters have long time to kills, or one meta that rules, creating boring and repeatitive cycles that quickly get old, while in siege, the vast cast of characters allow for different stratergies to come about, but if they fail, a round can be won through pure skill and induvisual hero plays.
Id compare this to CSGO, where the time to kill is also very fast, and the game is unplayable without proper utility usage and teamwork. People get creative with stratergy, placement, and compositions, creating exciting matches, and thriving competitive scenes.
